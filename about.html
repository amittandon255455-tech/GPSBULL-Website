We solve with technology

Nine years ago we set out to make every vehicle in India visible, measurable and safer. Today GPS Bull hardware tracks more than 2,50,000 vehicles every single day.

Who we are

An engineering company first, a vendor second

GPS Bull is the telematics brand of Triple7Bull Innovations Private Limited, based in New Delhi. We are a team of hardware, embedded and cloud engineers who design intelligent devices around satellite navigation and communication technologies — and manufacture them ourselves.

Because we own everything from PCB layout to the tracking platform, our clients get one accountable partner instead of a chain of resellers. That's how a device fault gets a firmware fix in days, not a support ticket that dies in someone else's queue.

We work with fleet operators, OEMs, state transport authorities and system integrators across India and 7+ countries.

Our expertise

Hardware to cloud, under one roof

  • Hardware & PCB design — multi-layer boards engineered for automotive vibration, temperature and voltage transients.
  • Real-time embedded firmware — RTOS-based applications with OTA update pipelines and watchdog-grade reliability.
  • Power-optimised design — devices that sip microamps in sleep and survive weeks on internal backup batteries.
  • Automotive-grade design — AIS-140, AIS-018 and legal-metrology compliance engineered in from the first schematic.
  • IoT cloud platform — CMMI Level 3 software practice behind the tracking, MIS and alerting stack.
