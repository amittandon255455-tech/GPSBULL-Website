Taxi-Auto Fare Meter

A DIMTS-approved digital fare meter with GPS and GSM built in — accurate fares passengers can trust, printed receipts, and trip data the operator and the transport authority can both verify.

Get a Quote Full Specifications
  • DIMTS approved

Why this meter

Fair fares, provable trips

Mechanical meters invite disputes and tampering. Our digital meter measures distance by GPS and wheel-pulse together, applies the authority-notified tariff exactly — day, night, waiting and luggage — and prints a receipt the passenger can keep.

Because GSM is built in, fares and trips sync to the operator dashboard automatically, and tariff revisions are pushed over the air to the whole fleet on the day the notification takes effect.

  • DIMTS approval — accepted for fitment under Delhi transport authority requirements.
  • OTA tariff updates — fare revisions deployed remotely, no workshop queue.
  • Tamper evidence — sealed housing and event logs; every open, reset and calibration is recorded.
  • Fleet dashboard — trips, fares, kilometres and shift summaries per vehicle and driver.
