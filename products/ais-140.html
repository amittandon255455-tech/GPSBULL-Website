Why AIS-140

Compliance you install once and forget

AIS-140 is mandatory for public service vehicles and national-permit commercial transport across India. The catch: devices must carry a valid Type Approval Certificate, use an embedded multi-operator eSIM, and report to government backends in the prescribed format — or your vehicles fail fitness inspection.

Our device ships pre-configured for your state's backend, with the TAC paperwork and fitment certificates handled by us. Beyond compliance, the same device gives you full commercial fleet tracking on the GPS Bull platform.

  • Emergency SOS — wired panic buttons raise alerts to the state emergency response backend and your control room simultaneously.
  • Dual reporting — positions stream to the government backend and the GPS Bull platform in parallel, no extra hardware.
  • Tamper resilience — internal battery, power-disconnection alerts and sealed housing keep the device reporting even when mains is cut.
  • Fleet features included — geofencing, trip history, over-speed alerts and MIS reports on web and mobile.
