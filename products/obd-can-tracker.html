Plug it into the OBD-II port and your vehicle starts talking: engine RPM, fuel level, coolant temperature, fault codes and driver behaviour — alongside full GPS tracking. No wiring, no workshop visit.

What it reads

The vehicle's own data, finally visible

A wired tracker tells you where the vehicle is. The OBD-CAN tracker tells you how it's running and how it's being driven — straight from the engine control unit, with no sensors to install.

Fault codes surface before breakdowns. Real fuel data ends pilferage disputes. Driver scores turn coaching from opinion into evidence.

  • Engine vitals — RPM, coolant temperature, engine load, battery voltage and odometer.
  • Fuel intelligence — tank level, consumption rate and refuel/drain events from CAN data.
  • Diagnostics (DTC) — fault codes pushed to the dashboard the moment the ECU raises them.
  • Driver behaviour — harsh braking, rash acceleration, sharp cornering and over-speed events, scored per trip.
  • Plus everything a tracker does — live location, geofences, trip history and alerts.

Specifications

Technical specifications

InstallationPlug-and-play into the standard OBD-II (SAE J1962) port; extension harness for concealed fitment
ProtocolsOBD-II (ISO 9141, ISO 14230/KWP2000, ISO 15765/CAN), SAE J1939 for commercial vehicles
Vehicle dataRPM, vehicle speed, coolant temp, engine load, fuel level/rate, odometer, battery voltage, DTC codes
PositioningGPS + GLONASS, ~2.5 m CEP accuracy
Cellular4G LTE with 2G fallback; Bluetooth LE for accessories and local configuration
Motion sensing3-axis accelerometer + gyroscope for driving-event detection and unplug/tamper alerts
Backup batteryInternal cell reports unplug events with last known position
Reporting10 s moving (configurable); offline buffering with automatic replay
Power drawPowered from OBD port; deep-sleep mode protects the vehicle battery when parked
CompatibilityCars, vans, LCVs and trucks built after 2008 (OBD-II mandate); J1939 trucks and buses
PlatformGPS Bull web + mobile apps, driver scorecards, maintenance alerts, REST API
