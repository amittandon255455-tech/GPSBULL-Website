GPS Tracker

The fleet workhorse: a compact, concealed, wired tracker that reports every movement in real time — with geofencing, remote immobilisation and a battery that keeps talking even when thieves cut the power.

Get a Quote Full Specifications
  • 3,00,000+ deployed
  • Any vehicle, 9–36 V

Capabilities

Everything a fleet needs from a tracker

Real-time tracking

Live positions every 10 seconds while moving, with 12 months of trip history and route playback.

Geofencing

Unlimited zones with entry/exit alerts — depots, client sites, no-go areas and state borders.

Remote immobilisation

Cut the fuel pump or starter via relay from the app — recover stolen vehicles before they leave the city.

Ignition & power sensing

Ignition on/off events, towing detection and instant alerts if the main power line is tampered with.

Network resilience

4G with 2G fallback and offline buffering — trips recorded in dead zones upload automatically.

Sensor expansion

Add fuel probes, temperature sensors, RFID driver ID or door switches on the same device.

Specifications

Technical specifications

PositioningGPS + GLONASS, ~2.5 m CEP accuracy; assisted GPS for fast cold start
Cellular4G LTE with 2G fallback; standard or M2M SIM (supplied and managed by GPS Bull)
Reporting10 s moving / 5 min stationary (configurable); ~5,000-record offline buffer
InputsIgnition sense, SOS (optional), door/temperature/fuel sensor inputs, 1-Wire for driver ID
OutputsRelay output for immobilisation (fuel pump / starter cut)
BatteryInternal backup battery — continues reporting after power cut, with tamper alert
Motion sensing3-axis accelerometer: towing, vibration and harsh-driving detection
Power9–36 V DC, surge and reverse-polarity protected; <2 mA sleep current
ProtectionIP65 enclosure (IP67 variant available); −20 °C to +70 °C
SizePalm-sized, concealable behind the dashboard; no external antenna required
PlatformGPS Bull web + mobile apps, alerts via push/SMS/email, REST API access
