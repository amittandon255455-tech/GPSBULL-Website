Speed Governor

An AIS-018 compliant speed limiting device that holds commercial vehicles at the legal limit — tamper-proof, RTO-acceptable, and engineered so drivers feel a governed vehicle, not a strangled one.

Why a governor

Compliance that also pays for itself

Speed limiting devices are mandatory for most commercial transport categories in India under AIS-018. But the business case stands on its own: governed fleets see fewer severe accidents, lower fuel burn at highway speeds, and reduced engine and brake wear.

Our governor controls the engine electronically — no crude fuel-line restriction — so the vehicle drives normally below the set limit and simply won't accelerate past it.

  • AIS-018 compliant — with the fitment certificate RTOs require at registration and fitness renewal.
  • Smooth limiting — progressive control near the threshold, no jerky cutoffs that unsettle loaded vehicles.
  • Tamper-proof — sealed unit with bypass detection; tampering is logged and visibly evident at inspection.
  • Fleet integration — pair with a GPS Bull tracker to see set-limit vs actual speed across the fleet.
