Engineering services

The same teams that build our own products take on client engineering — from a single PCB to a complete connected-product programme.

What we build

Five disciplines, one accountable team

Most telematics vendors integrate someone else's hardware. We design it — and we'll design yours too.

Automotive Designs

Electronics engineered for the harshest environment there is: a moving vehicle. Load-dump protection, reverse-polarity tolerance, conformal coating and vibration-tested mounting — designed to AIS and CE norms from day one.

Internet of Things (IoT) Solutions

End-to-end connected products: sensor selection, multi-network connectivity (4G/NB-IoT/BLE), device management, OTA pipelines and the cloud dashboard your operations team will actually use.

Hardware PCB Design

Schematic capture to production-ready Gerbers: multi-layer layout, EMI/EMC pre-compliance, DFM review with our own manufacturing line, and prototype-to-pilot turnaround measured in weeks.

Power Optimized Design

Battery-first engineering for assets that can't be wired: dynamic sleep scheduling, microamp standby currents, smart charging and power budgets validated on the bench — so trackers last weeks, not days.

Real-Time Embedded Applications

RTOS-based firmware with deterministic behaviour: CAN/OBD protocol stacks, sensor fusion, on-device analytics and fail-safe watchdog architecture — plus a CI pipeline that ships signed OTA updates.
