Why AIS-140

One standard behind three compliance regimes

AIS-140 is the Government of India standard for vehicle location tracking. Across transport, mining and food distribution, the same certified device is what makes a vehicle legal to run.

Certified hardware

A 4G VLTD with a valid Type Approval Certificate, embedded eSIM and emergency SOS — the device every backend will accept.

Backend registration

We register each device to the correct state and central backend — VAHAN, mining and FCI VLTS — so it reports in the prescribed format.

Live, audit-ready data

Real-time location, geofences, trip records and alerts — the evidence trail that satisfies an RTO inspector, a mining check-gate or an FCI audit.

RTO passing & fitness

Clear RTO fitness the first time

No commercial vehicle gets a fitness certificate without a working, backend-registered AIS-140 device. We make that step disappear — device, eSIM, VAHAN registration and the fitment certificate, all from one desk.

  1. Fitment. Certified VLTD installed and sealed by our trained technicians, with the fitment certificate issued on the spot.
  2. Backend registration. Device activated on the state VLTD backend and mapped to the vehicle on VAHAN / Parivahan.
  3. VAHAN fitness. Live device data validates against the backend so the fitness (CFx) test clears without a re-visit.
  4. CF issued. Vehicle is road-legal and already streaming to your fleet dashboard — compliance and visibility in one move.

FCI foodgrain movement

From depot to fair price shop, nothing goes missing

FCI's VLTS tracks subsidised grain in real time to stop diversion and black-marketing. Our AIS-140 devices geofence depots and shops, watch the route, and flag deviations or unscheduled stops the moment they happen.

  1. Loaded & sealed. Truck loads at the FCI depot; geofence exit timestamps the dispatch and the seal is logged.
  2. Route monitored. Live tracking against the assigned route — deviations, long halts and night movement raise instant alerts.
  3. Delivered & verified. Geofenced arrival at the fair price shop confirms delivery with an intact seal and an audit-ready trip log.
